Why should I use OneDB?

From a site owner point of view

OneDB is open source, meaning that you have full access to it's source code. After your website is ready, you can easily hire a web developer to extend it's functionality (although you probably won't need that becase it is very probably that those features are just right there implemented for you).

From a site administrator point of view

It has a very intuitive interface, and it was built with ergonomy, multitasking features, and performance in mind.

From a programmer point of view

After you'll take a look at the features of OneDB, you'll just simply love it.

It brings you the power and flexibility you would have building a site from the ground, but in the same time it makes your life easy so you won't even notice when your job is ready.
